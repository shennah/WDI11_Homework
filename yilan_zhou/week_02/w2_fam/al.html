Ada Lovelace

Born: 10/10/1815

Death: 27/11/1852

Place of Birth: London, UK

Nickname: Enchantress of Numbers

Fact

- Her mentor Charles Babbage is know as the father of the computer and invented the difference engine.

- From her work, Ada is often considered to be the first computer programmer. She introduced the concept of looping.

- Her contributions to the field of computer science were not discovered until the 1950s.
